Our Mission

Every year, millions of lives are lost in India due to blood shortages. LifeBridge was created to connect blood donors with blood banks, ensuring that no patient dies waiting for blood. We believe that access to safe blood should never be a barrier to saving lives.

Why Blood Donation?

🩸

Every 2 seconds

Someone in India needs blood - for surgery, accident, childbirth, or disease treatment.

❤️

One donation saves 3 lives

Blood components (red cells, plasma, platelets) can help three different patients.

🏥

No substitute for blood

Blood cannot be manufactured - only generous donors can provide it.

✓

Safe & Simple

Blood donation is safe, takes only 45-60 minutes, and your body replaces blood quickly.
