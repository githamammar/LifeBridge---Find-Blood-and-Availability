Why Donate Blood?

  • ✓ Saves up to 3 lives per donation
  • ✓ Blood cannot be manufactured - only donated
  • ✓ Every 2 seconds, someone needs blood
  • ✓ Safe and simple process (45-60 minutes)
✅

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✓ Age: 18-65 years
  • ✓ Weight: > 45 kg
  • ✓ Hemoglobin: > 12.5 g/dL
  • ✓ No recent tattoos/piercings (6 months)
  • ✓ No major surgery in last 6 months
  • ✓ Not on certain medications

📋 How to Donate Blood

1️⃣ Find a blood bank near you using our Map or Directory
2️⃣ Call them to confirm timings and requirements
3️⃣ Visit the blood bank (carry ID proof)
4️⃣ Complete a quick health checkup
5️⃣ Donate blood (takes 10-15 minutes)
6️⃣ Rest and enjoy refreshments 🍪
7️⃣ You've saved lives! 🎉

💪 Benefits of Blood Donation

• Free health checkup (BP, hemoglobin, blood group)
• Reduces risk of heart disease
• Helps burn calories
• Makes you feel good about saving lives
• Many blood banks provide refreshments and small gifts
